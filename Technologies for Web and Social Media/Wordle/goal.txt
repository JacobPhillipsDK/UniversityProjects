Worlde - Brainstorm


Rules of worlde ---

Guess the WORDLE in six tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

-----------

Correct - Letter placement turns into green box
Yellow  - Correct Letter wrong placements
Gray    - Letter not being used.

-----------
User interface
- Boxgrid where words can be typed into
- Easy overview over what letters are used or not
- See how many tries are back
----------
Web app functions
Find Word from wordlist
Check if the Letters are in the designated word
Turn Green/Yellow/Gray based on letter
